Chapter 10: The Dragon's Servants (Violence Level 1)

As they approached the Obsidian Peak, shadow creatures emerged from the darkness—servants of the dragon, twisted beings of smoke and malice. Arden and Kael drew their weapons to defend themselves. Kael's sword met the creature's claw, metal ringing against something harder than stone.

She fought with discipline, using her training to deflect the attacks. The battle was intense but controlled. Kael moved through combat with precision, striking the creatures down with decisive sword strokes. The creatures fell, dissipating into ash. Some blood was spilled—mostly the creatures', some of Kael's from a shallow cut on her arm.

After the battle, both warriors were breathing hard, but the encounter, while dangerous, was manageable. Arden pressed a cloth to Kael's wound. "We can do this," he said quietly.
